about me

Turning Data into Stories, and Projects into Masterpieces

Welcome! I’m Preet Desai—a professional Data Analyst and Project Manager who believes that data isn’t just numbers; it’s the world’s most compelling storyteller. Growing up with a stockbroker father, I learned early on that numbers have a language of their own. They whisper trends, predict outcomes, and reveal secrets hidden in plain sight. While I initially pursued electrical engineering and dabbled in predictive maintenance using AI, I quickly realized my true passion lay in unraveling insights from data. Numbers may run in my genes, but I chose to give them a voice. To balance this technical side, I earned a Minor in Finance to understand the economic stories behind the data. During my time at UT Dallas, where I earned my MS in Business Analytics, I mastered the art of SQL, Python, R, and visualization tools like Tableau and Power BI to tackle real-world challenges. As a Graduate Teaching Assistant, I added flair to database indexing, proving that even the driest topics can become engaging (if you know how to tell the story right!).

Professionally, I’ve blended analytics with strategy to make an impact. My work has spanned crafting predictive models, developing strategies that drive growth, and building frameworks that enhance business continuity. I’ve worked on projects in diverse industries, from healthcare and sustainability to market analysis, proving my ability to adapt and highlighting my versatility and technical prowess. Whether it’s translating business requirements into technical solutions or coordinating cross-functional teams, I bring a balanced approach of strategic thinking and precision. But what’s life without a little kick? As a disciplined black belt in Martial Arts (Shito Ryu Karate), I don’t just tackle challenges; I break through them. Whether it’s delivering results under pressure or coordinating flawless events as UT Dallas’ Events Manager, I approach every task with precision, adaptability, and a collaborative spirit.

What drives me? Crafting innovative solutions that empower businesses to grow and customers to smile. I thrive in dynamic environments, blending tradition with innovation, much like the color white blends to create something uniquely vibrant. Oh, and if the situation calls for it, I can quite literally kick problems to the curb (martial-artist’s honor!). Let’s connect and explore how we can transform data into powerful solutions and write the next chapter of success—together.
